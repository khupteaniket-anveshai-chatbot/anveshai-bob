How to Use AnveshAI

Get the most out of your MPSC exam preparation

1

Browse Subjects

Start by exploring available subjects from the home page. Currently, we're focusing on Science, with more subjects coming soon. Click on any subject to see all available topics.

Go to Home
2

Select Topics

Within each subject, you'll find various topics (e.g., Physics, Chemistry, Biology for Science). Each topic contains multiple notes organized by difficulty and importance.

3

Filter & Find Notes

Use filters to find notes based on:

  • Difficulty: Easy, Medium, or Hard
  • Importance: 1 to 5 stars (⭐ to ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐)
  • Tags: Specific topics or categories

This helps you focus on notes that match your current preparation level and priorities.

4

Study the Notes

Each note contains:

  • Main Content: Comprehensive explanation of the topic
  • Key Points: Quick bullet points for revision
  • Exam Tips: Strategies and tricks for exam success
  • Common Mistakes: What to avoid during exams
5

Bookmark Important Notes

Click the Bookmark button on any note to save it for quick access later. View all your bookmarked notes from the Bookmarks page in the navigation menu.

View Bookmarks
6

Use Search Feature

Can't find what you're looking for? Use the search feature to find notes across all subjects and topics. You can search by keywords, topics, or specific content.

Search Notes
7

Check Hot Topics

Visit the Hot Topics section to see which topics appear most frequently in Previous Year Questions (PYQs). This helps you prioritize your preparation and focus on high-yield topics.

View Hot Topics

Pro Tips

  • Start with high-importance (⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐) topics first
  • Focus on exam tips and common mistakes sections
  • Bookmark notes you need to revise multiple times
  • Use filters to create custom study sessions
  • Check hot topics regularly to stay updated on important areas
  • Revise key points before exams for quick recall
